Ground Truth = Crowdsourced Segment <br>
Grabcut 1 = Edge mask + hard extreme points / centroid / skeleton <br>
Grabcut 2 = Edge mask + hard extreme points / skeleton <br>
DEXTR 1 = COCO Pretrained <br>
DEXTR 2 = COCO Pretrained + finetuned 1 epoch on 1.5K samples from Grabcut 1 <br>

DEXTR 1 and DEXTR 2 achieve ~ 76.5% mIOU <br>
Grabcut 1 and Grabcut 2 achieve ~ 72.4% mIOU <br>
